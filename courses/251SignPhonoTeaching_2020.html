Lecture handouts

  • Class 1 (Jan. 7): Why this course; basic background on sign languages and sign language phonology

  • Class 2 (Jan. 14): contrast and representation; markedness; processes
    • Blake presented Liddell & Johnson 1986 part I
    • Zhuo presented Liddell & Johnson 1986 part II
    • Jennifer presented Eccarius & Brentari 2007
    • Adam presented Morgan & Mayberry 2012
    • Kie presented Mandel 1979
    • Jinyoung presented Ormel & al. 2017

  • Class 3 (Jan. 21): contrast and representation; processes; process interaction
    • Tutorial on classifiers
    • Beth presented Brentari & Eccarius 2010
    • Maddy presented Battison 1974
    • Z.L. presented Padden & Perlmutter 1987
    • Zhuo presented Liddell & Johnson 1986 part III

  • Class 4 (Jan. 28): prosody, intonation
    • Kie presented Nespor & Sandler 1999, Sandler 1999
    • Beth presented Wilbur 1999
    • Zhuo presented Tang, Brentari, Gonzalez & Sze 2010
    • Adam presented Crasborn & van der Kooij 2013
    • Z.L. presented Perlmutter 1993
    • Adam presented Ormel & Crasborn 2012

  • Class 5 (Feb. 4): morphology; free choice example-mining
    • Maddy presented Sandler 1993
    • Maddy presented Pfau & Steinbach 2011
    • Blake presented Meir, Aronoff, Sandler & Padden 2010
    • Blake mined Nyst 2007 for Adamorobe Sign Language examples
    • Z.L. mined Wilbur 2000 for nonmanual examples
    • Beth mined Johnston & Schembri 2007 for Australian Sign Language examples

  • Class 6 (Feb. 11): more free-choice example-mining; variation
    • Kie gave highlights of unpresented papers from previous topics: Jantunen & Takkinen 2010, van der Kooij 2002, Mak & Tang 2011, Eccarius 2011
    • Adam mined Barberà & Mosella 2014
    • Zhuo mined Tang 2006
    • Jinyoung mined Fischer & Gong 2010
    • Adam presented Tamminga, Fisher & Hochgesang 2020

  • Class 7 (Feb. 18): iconicity, diachronic change, acquisition
    • Maddy presented Wilbur 2010
    • Blake presented Frishberg 1979
    • Zhuo presented Morgan, Barrett-Jones & Stoneham 2007
    • Beth presented Petitto & Marentette 1991
    • Z.L. presented Brentari 2019
    • Kie gave highlights of unpresented papers from previous topics: Schuit 2012; Mathur & Rathmann 2006; Mathur & Rathmann 2010

  • Class 8 (Feb. 25): processing, poetry, loans and contact, more variation
    • Kie gave highlights of three psycholinguistics overview articles: Brentari 2019; Corina, Gutierrez & Grosvald 2014; Emmorey 2009
    • Blake presented Klima & Bellugi 1979
    • Adam presented Sutton-Spence 2005
    • Z.L. presented Crasborn 2006
    • Zhuo presented Crasborn, van der Kooij, Waters, Woll & Mensch 2008
    • Maddy presented Miller 2001
    • Kie presented McCaskill, Lucas, Bayley & Hill 2011, and we watched part of the DVD that comes with the book

  • Class 9 (Mar. 3): miscellaneous
    • Blake presented Morgan 2017
    • Beth presented Baker 2017
    • Kie gave highlights of unpresented papers from previous topics: Ann 1996, Sandler 1999, Frishberg 1975, Wilcox & Occhino 2016

  • Class 10 (Mar. 10): miscellaneous
    • Kie gave highlights of unpresented papers from previous topics: Karnopp 2002; Hendriks & Dufoe 2014; Cormier, Schemri & Tyrone 2008; Goldin-Meadow & Brentari 2015; selections from Boyes-Braem & Sutton-Spence 2001 volume (Vogt-Svendsen 2001, Rainò 2001, Bergman & Wallin 2001, Woll 2001)
    • Adam presented Napoli & Sutton-Spence 2010
    • Maddy presented Klima & Bellugi 1976
    • Blake presented Clark 2017
    • Beth presented Sinha 2018
    • Z.L. presented a problem set on rule/process interaction
